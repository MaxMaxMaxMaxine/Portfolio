01 . The Problem

The Situation

Group plans break down quickly.

Deciding what to do as a group often turns into long message threads with no clear outcome. There are no existing non-sticky tools to support group decision-making during group travel.


They rely on:

  • Back-and-forth conversations
  • Manual coordination
  • Uneven contribution


This leads to slow decision-making and in some cases, conflict.

02 . The Goal

Effortless Decision-Making

  • Create a tool that takes group dynamics into account
  • Help groups reduce friction
  • Create a non-sticky tool that doesn't take them out of their travel experience

03 . User Research

What did the backpackers think?

  • Interviewed 30 backpackers
  • Aged 18–24
  • Across 2 different hostels


We found that:

  • Hidden power dynamic in group planning
  • Planning responsibilities and roles are unclear
  • Decision-making is exhausting for everyone
  • Different planning styles = Conflict
  • Everyone wants a fun, stress-free group outing
